How to use Nano Banana

A short guide with examples. Open the bot and follow the steps below.

1. Upload a photo

Send a single source photo to the chat. The bot will save it and prompt you to enter a text request (prompt).

2. Enter a prompt

For example:

“Make a warm-toned comic style”
“Add a soft glow and a studio background”

3. Results

The bot will return a ready image. A few examples:

Prompt: “Dress in a suit”
Prompt: “Add a hoodie and hair”
Prompt: “Change the watch to a toy”

Tips

  • Try to be specific in your prompts (style, tone, background, details).
  • If you run out of credits — open “Buy credits” or use a referral code.
  • Complex prompts may take longer — please wait for the bot’s reply.
  • First upload a photo, then send your prompt as a separate message.
  • If the bot shows “Oops, something went wrong. Please try again later.”, try again or tweak the prompt.
  • If credits were mistakenly charged for an invalid generation, contact support @lechat_Alexey.
